Saw Fanatic!!

I have been a huuuuuuuuge fan of the Saw films since the first one was released on the 1st October 2004.
I mean who doesn't love a bit of gore?!.....and the Saw films are jam packed with it, especially when it comes to the 'traps'.

I love how Jigsaw creates these scenarios/traps which are based on his 'subjects' flaws...the way he does this by making a life or death situation fascinates me, it's like a case of 'how much do you value you your life' and 'are you willing to put yourself through extremities to free yourself.'

My favourite Saw traps.

When it comes to the traps Jigsaw uses for the 'players' of the game, they are definitely awesome. There are a number of traps throughout the Saw franchise and most of them have definitely given me goosebumps and had me glued to the screen.

.....but if i had to choose my top ten, then these would be them:

  1. The Pendulum Trap(Saw V)
  2. The Angel Trap(Saw III)
  3. The Horsepower Trap(Saw 3D)
  4. The Classroom Trap(Saw III)
  5. The Subway Trap(Spiral)Had to include this as it was rather awesome
  6. Pound of Flesh(Saw VI)
  7. The Rack(Saw III)
  8. The Furnace(Saw II)
  9. The Antidote Safe(Saw II)
  10. Hand Trap(Saw II)
A little refresher on the Saw traps.


Rating of Spiral and Jigsaw

I'll have to admit, I enjoyed both films, they both had their added twists...which was enjoyable. I would have to say I preferred Jigsaw over Spiral

The only reason why, is because it has more of a connection to the previous Saw films....trap wise both were awesome, from the Finger Trap to the Subway Trap(which I absolutely loved)
